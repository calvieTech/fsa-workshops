About

Hi there! My name is Calvin and I'm excited to connect with you. As a Full Stack Developer, I've had the opportunity to work with startups, non-profit organizations, church groups, small, medium and large corporations to help build and scale their online presence. My love for computers and technology in general led me to specialize in developing well-polished online presences for businesses and organizations. I've learned a ton through lots of trial & error, painful debugging sessions, and countless cups of coffee, and have gained expertise in front-end, back-end, and software development in general. I'm passionate about delivering real value to businesses and organizations, and I've been able to achieve this by teaching real-world projects that promote growth and foster an amazing online presence. I'm eager to bring my skills and enthusiasm to your team and help you achieve your goals. Thanks for considering my application, and I look forward to discussing how I can contribute to your team.
